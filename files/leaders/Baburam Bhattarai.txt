                    Baburam Bhattarai

Baburam Bhattarai is a Nepalese politician and federal 
Chairman of Janta Samajbadi party who was Prime Minister of 
Nepal from August 2011 to March 2013. He was a long-time 
leading member of the Unified Communist Party of Nepal 
(Maoist) prior to founding a new party, Naya Shakti. 

Objectives:
If I am elected as prime minister of Nepal, I would accomplish 
the following objectives in my tenure to become a successful 
prime minister:

1.  Upgrade the country into a developing nation by ending 
    country's poverty within five years.
2.  Achieve the sustainable development goals in coming 15  
    years.
3.  More investment in agriculture, tourism, energy and 
    medicinal herbs.
4.  Take the country into the middle-income country and end 
    unemployment.
