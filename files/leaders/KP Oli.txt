                        Khadga Prasad Sharma Oli

Khadga Prasad Sharma Oli is a Nepalese politician and former 
Prime Minister of Nepal. He served three terms as prime 
minister from 11 October 2015 to 3 August 2016, from 15 
February 2018 to 13 May 2021 as the first elected prime 
minister under the new constitution, and from 13 May 2021 to 
13 July 2021.

Objectives:
If I am elected as prime minister of Nepal, I would accomplish 
the following objectives in my tenure to become a successful 
prime minister:

1.  Increase the per capita income to US $ 5,000 within 10 
    years.
2.  Generate additional 15,000 megawatt electricity in the 
    next one decade through several projects.
3.  Operate modern tram and metro train services in the inner 
    Ring road of Kathmandu and extend train services to 
    adjoining districts.
4.  Create a mega city incorporating Kathmandu, Bhaktapur and 
    Lalitpur districts of Kathmandu Valley.
