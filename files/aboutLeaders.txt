                    Puspa Kamal Dahal

Pushpa Kamal Dahal, also widely known by his Prachanda, 
meaning "fierce", is a Nepalese politician who served as Prime 
Minister of Nepal from 2008 to 2009 and again from 2016 to 
2017. Currently he is the president of CPN Maoist. 

Objectives:
If I am elected as prime minister of Nepal, I would accomplish 
the following objectives in my tenure to become a successful 
prime minister:
1.  Develop the Kathmandu Valley as an integrated town.
2.  Ensure nationality, democracy, unity, peace, stability    
    and development in the nation.
3.  Alleviation of poverty, employment, healthy environment 
    and fulfilling the millennium development goals.


                        
                        Khadga Prasad Sharma Oli

Khadga Prasad Sharma Oli is a Nepalese politician and former 
Prime Minister of Nepal. He served three terms as prime 
minister from 11 October 2015 to 3 August 2016, from 15 
February 2018 to 13 May 2021 as the first elected prime 
minister under the new constitution, and from 13 May 2021 to 
13 July 2021.

Objectives:
If I am elected as prime minister of Nepal, I would accomplish 
the following objectives in my tenure to become a successful 
prime minister:

1.  Increase the per capita income to US $ 5,000 within 10 
    years.
2.  Generate additional 15,000 megawatt electricity in the 
    next one decade through several projects.
3.  Operate modern tram and metro train services in the inner 
    Ring road of Kathmandu and extend train services to 
    adjoining districts.
4.  Create a mega city incorporating Kathmandu, Bhaktapur and 
    Lalitpur districts of Kathmandu Valley.
    
                    
                    
                    Baburam Bhattarai

Baburam Bhattarai is a Nepalese politician and federal 
Chairman of Janta Samajbadi party who was Prime Minister of 
Nepal from August 2011 to March 2013. He was a long-time 
leading member of the Unified Communist Party of Nepal 
(Maoist) prior to founding a new party, Naya Shakti. 

Objectives:
If I am elected as prime minister of Nepal, I would accomplish 
the following objectives in my tenure to become a successful 
prime minister:

1.  Upgrade the country into a developing nation by ending 
    country's poverty within five years.
2.  Achieve the sustainable development goals in coming 15  
    years.
3.  More investment in agriculture, tourism, energy and 
    medicinal herbs.
4.  Take the country into the middle-income country and end 
    unemployment.
